Shaheed Diwas Run: Honoring the Brave, Running for Their Legacy

Presented by "Fit World Association"

Join the Shaheed Diwas Run on 23rd March 2025 to honor the supreme sacrifice of Shaheed Bhagat Singh, Rajguru, and Sukhdev.

This virtual run is a tribute to the courage, patriotism, and indomitable spirit of the martyrs who laid down their lives for India’s freedom.

Run, walk, or use a treadmill at your own pace and location to keep their legacy alive.

Event Details

📅 Date: 23rd March 2025

🌍 Mode: Virtual – Participate from anywhere, at your convenience

🏃 Categories: 3 km | 5 km | 10 km | 15 km | 21 km | 42 km

📍 Location: Any place of your choice

⏱ Track Your Run: Use any fitness app (Strava, Runkeeper, Nike Run Club, Google Fit, etc.)

🏅 E-Certificate: Free for all finishers, delivered via email within 1-2 working days

Medals & T-shirts

🎖 Medals & T-shirts available (for order) to commemorate your participation.

📦 Delivery: Within 10-14 working days after the event.

🔹 Special Offer: Contact the organizer for exclusive discounts.

📌 International Delivery: Available only in India. For international orders, contact the organizer.

Why Join?

✅ Honor the Martyrs: Pay tribute to Bhagat Singh, Rajguru, and Sukhdev.

✅ Run for a Cause: Keep their legacy alive by participating in this meaningful event.

✅ Free Recognition: Receive an e-certificate and get featured on social media.

✅ Join a Nationwide Movement: Be part of a collective effort to remember our heroes.

How to Participate

1️⃣ Register: Sign up for free on Townscript to confirm your participation.

2️⃣ Run on 23rd March 2025: Walk, run, or use a treadmill to complete at least 3 km (or more).

3️⃣ Submit Your Proof: Upload your run details (distance & time) via the submission link. Submit Here

4️⃣ Celebrate & Share: Tag us on Strava or Instagram using #ShaheedDiwasRun #RunForMartyrs #FitWorldAssociation to inspire others.

Get Social and Shine!

📸 Capture Your Moment: Take a photo or video during your run, proudly wearing or displaying a tribute to Shaheed Bhagat Singh, Rajguru, and Sukhdev. This is optional but encouraged to showcase your respect and remembrance.

📤 Submit Your Tribute Photo: When uploading your activity, you can also submit your photo to get featured on our social media platforms. Simply include your photo along with your activity screenshot via the submission link: Submit Your Activity Here

📢 Spread the Word: Tag us on Strava or Instagram with the hashtags #ShaheedDiwasRun, #TributeToMartyrs, #FitWorldAssociation, and #RunForFreedom to inspire others and honor the sacrifices of our heroes!
